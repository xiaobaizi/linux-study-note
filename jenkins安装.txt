安装
wget -O /etc/yum.repos.d/jenkins.repo https://pkg.jenkins.io/redhat-stable/jenkins.repo
rpm --import https://pkg.jenkins.io/redhat-stable/jenkins.io.key
yum install jenkins  -y 
下载地址：https://www.jenkins.io/zh/download/
systemctl start jenkins 
问题处理，/usr/bin/java没有这个目录，然后将jenkins目录修改下就可以
[root@centos]# systemctl daemon-reload
[root@centos]# systemctl restart jenkins
具体参考：https://www.jianshu.com/p/ed73f1f07682


复制密码到浏览器框里
[root@centos]# cat /var/lib/jenkins/secrets/initialAdminPassword
